Tko smo mi?

Who are we?

Mi smo tim koji radi uz Tehničko Veleučilište u Zagrebu koja želi promicati znanosti, tehnologije, inženjerstva i matematike (STEM) među djecom osnovnoškolskog uzrasta. Naš cilj je potaknuti kreativnost i logičko razmišljanje kroz interaktivne radionice koje prilagođavamo prema interesima djece.

We are a team which works alongside the Polytehhnic University of Zagreb whos aim is to promote science, technology, engineering and mathematics (STEM) among primary school aged children. Our goal is to encourage creativity and logical thinking through our interactive workshops that we've specifically designed to adapt to the interests of children.

Naše radionice osmišljene su tako da budu zabavne ali i edukativne. Kroz teme poput robotike, programiranja, eksperimentiranja i inženjerskih izazova, želimo pokazati da STEM nije samo za odrasle ili stručnjake nego da i djeca mogu biti inovatori, izumitelji i istraživači.

Our workshops are designed to be fun as well as educational. Through topics such as robotics, programming, experimentation and engineering challenges, we want to show that STEM isn't just for adults or experts but that children can also be innovators, inventors and explorers.

Suradnju imamo s brojnim školama, znanstvenim institucijama i stručnjacima koji dijele našu viziju o boljem budućnosti obrazevanja. Posebnu pažnju posvećujemo inkluzivnosti i omogućujemo sudjelovanje djeci svih mogućnosti i iz različitih zajednica.

We collaborate with numerous schools, scientific institutions and experts who share our vision for a better future of education. We also pay special attention to inclusivity and enable the participation of children of all abilities and from diverse communities.

Naša udruga je više od radionice. Mi smo zajednica roditelja, mentora i djece koja zajedno gradi svijet u kojem je znanje dostupno, zanimljivo i korisno svakome tko želi učiti.

Our organization is more than just a workshop. We are a community of parents, mentors and children who work togheter to build a world where knowledge is accessible, interesting and useful to anyone who wants to learn.

Postani Volonter!

Become a Volunteer!

Zanima vas volontiranje?

Interest in volunteering?

Veselimo se uključiti volontera koji dijele naš entuzijazam prema STEM i rad s djecom. Volonteri trebaju imati osnovne komunikacijske vještine, volju za učenjem i spremnost na timski rad. Obrazac za prijavu je dostupan odmah. Ako želite postati dio našeg tima navedi vaše ime i prezime, e-mail, vaše vještine koje su relevantne i dostupnost. Pridružite se nam u stvaranju inspirativnog okruženja za buduće inovatore!

We are looking forward to involving volunteers who share our enthusiasm for STEM and working with children. Volunteers should have basic communication skills, a want to learn and the willingness to work in a team. The application form is available now. If you'd like to become a part of our team provide your name and surname, e-mail, relevant skills and availability. Join us in creating an inspiring enviornment for future innovators!
